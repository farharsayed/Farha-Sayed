Mission Statement

We are a student-led business in Leeds that is in the process of creating a small quantitative hedge fund.

By utilising our knowledge in econometrics and quantitative programs,
we seek to grow the long-term capital of our investors sustainably.

What We Do

We offer a low-cost and low-risk option for investors to invest their capital.

We also provide a different approach to investing, whereas most quant funds only use predictive algorithms for capital allocation.

We plan to use a combination of fundamental methods and predictive algorithms, especially regarding risk management.

We also want to use our position in the university to utilise the knowledge of students in the Leeds Business School.
